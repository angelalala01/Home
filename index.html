Why NAMRIAkbay?

NAMRIAkbay is rooted in the essential role of NAMRIA as the National Mapping and Resource Information Authority of the Philippines. NAMRIA serves as the primary government agency responsible for providing mapping services, managing geospatial data, and supporting various national development initiatives through the provision of accurate and up-to-date information.

Who is NAMRIA?

The National Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA) is a government agency responsible for providing the public with mapmaking services and acting as the central mapping agency, depository, and distribution facility of natural resources data in the form of maps, charts, texts, and statistics. As the central mapping agency of the government, NAMRIA plays a crucial role in promoting national development by providing accurate and reliable geospatial data to various stakeholders.
